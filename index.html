What Is OddVoter?

Our mission is simple, to make voting a habit. We believe that by actively participating in local discourse, citizens will be inclined to actively participate in even the most minor city ballots.

We aim to promote active civic engagement by enabling citizens to easily cast their opinion on local trending issues presented by their elected officials.
